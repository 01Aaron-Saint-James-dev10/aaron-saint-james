About

Designed by lived experience.

I'm a neurodivergent researcher, founder and advocate working where accessibility, Universal Design for Learning and inclusive education meet. I founded Simplifii to give students with ADHD and dyslexia study support that works with how their minds work — and my research (Master of Research, UNSW Sydney) maps how universities can make inclusion real, not just stated.

Everything I build is grounded in lived experience and co-production: nothing about us, without us.
