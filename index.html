About

Designed by lived experience.

I'm a neurodivergent researcher, founder and advocate working where accessibility, Universal Design for Learning and inclusive education meet. I founded Simplifii to give students with ADHD and dyslexia study support that works with how their minds work.

My research — a Master of Research at UNSW Sydney — maps how Australian universities can make inclusion real, not just stated. Everything I build is grounded in co-production: nothing about us, without us.
